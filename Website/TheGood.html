Malakai is adorable


As you can see Malakai is a very cute cat. He has a lovely loud purr and likes his tummy being touched/combed.

He is also quite good at coming to you when you call his name, unless he's intent on hunting some mouse or bird outside.

It's nice that he will willingly jump on my lap if I'm sat down and will stay on it for a while, whether or not i'm giving him attention.
